Improve the functionality of your home

Flooring is excellent for protecting concrete from humidity, stains and heavy objects. EvergGreen Construction materials can be used in all kinds of environments, from epoxy flooring to commercial environments. The word flooring is the general term for a permanent floor covering or for the construction of such a floor covering. Floor covering is a term used to describe any finishing product added to a floor structure for a walking surface. All terms are used interchangeably, but the floor covering more explicitly applies to materials.
